Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data Viralia collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Your email address, collected when you sign in via Firebase Authentication.

Conversation Content: The text of customer support conversations conducted through Viralia. This content is transmitted to Groq, Inc.'s API servers for AI processing in real time during active sessions. Conversation history is stored locally on your device after sessions end.

Profile Data (Optional): Name, email address, billing address, and last 4 digits of a payment card — only if you choose to enter them in My Profile. This data is stored exclusively on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption backed by the Android Keystore. It is never transmitted to Viralia's servers.

App Usage Data: Basic crash reports and anonymized usage analytics to improve the product (via Firebase Crashlytics/Analytics).

Device Information: Android OS version, app version, and device model — used for bug diagnosis only.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core AI writing assistance service (requires Groq API).
  • To authenticate your account securely via Firebase.
  • To store your session history locally on your device for your reference.
  • To improve our AI prompts and service quality using aggregated, anonymized usage data.
  • To send push notifications about your active sessions (only with your permission).

3. Data Sharing & How Viralia Makes Money

Groq, Inc. (AI API provider): Conversation content is sent to Groq's servers in real time for AI response generation. Groq processes this data under their own Privacy Policy (groq.com/privacy).

Google LLC / Firebase: Your email address is stored with Firebase Authentication. Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics may collect anonymized usage and crash data.

Aggregate Market Intelligence (ANONYMIZED — NO PERSONAL DATA):

Viralia's business model includes licensing fully anonymized, aggregated, non-personally-identifiable market insights to third parties.

What we may collect and license:

  • Aggregated statistics about which categories of consumer issues are most common
  • Industry-level resolution rate benchmarks
  • Anonymized negotiation effectiveness patterns by company category
  • Aggregate session metadata: duration, message count, resolution outcome

What we NEVER include:

  • Your name, email address, or any identifier
  • The actual text of any conversation
  • Your account numbers, card details, or profile data
  • Any data that could be linked back to you as an individual
  • IP addresses, device IDs, or location

No Other Sharing: We do NOT sell your personal data. We do NOT sell conversation transcripts. We do NOT share your identity with advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data We Do Not Share

  • We do not share your conversations (actual text) with any third party.
  • We do not sell personal data to any third party.
  • We do not use your data to serve targeted advertisements.
  • We do not share your data with the retailers or businesses you contact through Viralia.

5. Data Storage & Security

Session history and profile data are stored locally on your device, encrypted using AES-256-GCM via Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences backed by the Android Keystore. This data is not uploaded to Viralia's servers. We implement industry-standard security practices for data in transit (TLS/HTTPS for all API calls).

6. Data Retention

  • Local device data (session history, profile) is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Firebase Authentication data is retained until you delete your account.
  • To request deletion: privacy@viralia.app

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data, and to withdraw consent. Contact: privacy@viralia.app

8. California Residents — CCPA/CPRA

We do not sell personal information. California residents may submit rights requests to: privacy@viralia.app

9. European Users — GDPR

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, your data is processed under GDPR. Legal basis: consent and legitimate interest. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account. GDPR requests: privacy@viralia.app

10. Children's Privacy

Viralia is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Push Notifications

You may disable notifications at any time in your device's system Settings or within the Viralia app Settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notice.

13. Contact

Email: privacy@viralia.app
Support: support@viralia.app
